Things We Do

We specialize in live event cinematography and photography. We've worked with countless companies around the country including DoLabs, Insomniac, Qdrinks, and more.

48-Hour Turn Around

At Teamsushie, we believe you should NEVER have to wait for your images. You're just as excited as we are! That's why we have a 48-hour turn around policy. This means you'll have your images within the next 2 business days.

From composition to posing we have your back with a combined 5.5 years of photo experience. Our priority is to provide you with beautiful and eye-catching edits.
